Here's a short time line of Prof. J B Peterson's life:

  • 1962. Jordan Bernt Peterson was born on 12th June 1962 in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada
  • 1984. Graduated from the University of Alberta, with a BA in political science in 1984.
  • 1989. Peterson married Tammy Roberts in 1989, with whom he has a daughter (Mikhaila) and a son (Julian).
  • 2013. Peterson registered a YouTube channel named JordanPetersonVideos
  • 2022. Peterson signed a deal with the news company The Daily Wire, which includes the distribution rights to Peterson's video and podcast library.
